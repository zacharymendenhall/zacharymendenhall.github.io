Zachary Mendenhall is a Chicago-based Film Composer and Singer. He has most recently finished the music for the critically acclaimed Island's of Sina Una's Kickstarter, along with Metal Weave Games' Libations.

Zachary can also be remembered for his work in Soulless', composing alongside Christian King.

Zachary initially studied under the direction of Dr. Brandon Rumsey at Madonna University in 2018. There, Zachary received scholarships and merits for his work on The Eagle, a composition he had finished for his admission into Madonna University's Film Scoring program.
