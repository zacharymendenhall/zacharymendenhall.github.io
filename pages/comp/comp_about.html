Zachary Mendenhall is a Chicago-based Film Composer and Singer. Mendenhall most recently worked on Polyhedral 2.0, writing music for its' feature trailer. He composed the music for the critically acclaimed Island's of Sina Una's Kickstarter, along with Metal Weave Games' Libations.

Zachary can also be remembered for his work in Soulless', composing alongside Christian King.

Zachary studies under the direction of Dr. Brandon Rumsey, since meeting with them at Madonna University in 2018. There, Mendenhall received scholarships and merits for his work on The Eagle, a composition he had finished for his admission into Madonna University's Film Scoring program. Because of his work, he received a merit award of $24,000 before moving to Chicago, IL.
